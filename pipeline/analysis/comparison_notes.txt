What the graphs show about the upgrade:
  Panel 1 reveals a structurally different departure-time profile: the upgraded model shows a clear 7–9 AM morning peak driven by the Commuter profile constraint, while the NHTS baseline distributes departures more uniformly across the day (KS statistic 0.36, Wasserstein distance 3.45 h from the ComparisonAB report).
