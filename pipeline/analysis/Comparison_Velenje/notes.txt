  - MECHANISM (confirmed from run log): for Work/Education/Business in Velenje,
    the precise annular isochrone zone (floor-to-ceil trip duration) is empty
    37/100 times (100% of Education trips, 56% of Work trips) because Velenje has
    few tagged buildings of those types in ABSOLUTE terms (work=74, edu=19
    citywide). When empty, ors_isochrone_filter falls back to the FULL isochrone
    from 0 minutes, and the gravity model's distance decay (beta=2, ~1/d^2) then
    heavily favours whatever is nearest in that wide pool, regardless of budget.

    incomplete OSM data' and 'Velenje is the smaller town' explanations were BOTH

    vs 0.41/km^2). Per SURS (stat.si/obcine, mid-2023/2024): Velenje municipality
    has 33,680 people on 84 km^2 (401/km^2, ~4x national average) -- MORE people
    than all of Krsko municipality (26,070 on 287 km^2, 91/km^2, below average).
    Velenje settlement is Slovenia's 6th largest town; Krsko town isn't top 10.
  - REAL CAUSE: Velenje is smaller by MUNICIPALITY LAND AREA (84 vs 287 km^2),
    not by population or OSM density. The small area caps how many distinct
    Work/Education buildings can exist, even when densely packed. This is a
    believable feature of Velenje's compact, dense urban form, not a data flaw.
